## Printing with Bambu Studio
First, download the Bambu Studio slicer [HERE](https://bambulab.com/en-us/download/studio)

Once you have logged into the Bambu account, printing is as easy as slicing your .stl file, and pressing the green *Print* button in the top right corner.

## Printing with the SD card
If for whatever reason wireless printing is not working, you can also print using the SD card. The SD card is located on the right side of the screen. There is a USB adapter on the right side of the printer. Just slice the .stl file, press File > Export > Export Plate Sliced File.

Save the resulting .3mf file to the SD card and eject the USB. Plug the SD card back into the printer, and wait about 90 seconds for the printer to recognize the card. Once the card is recognized, select the file you wish to print, the AMS slot you wish to use, and print.

## Using Bambu Handy
Bambu Handy is a Android/iOS app that allows for remote monitoring and printing.
