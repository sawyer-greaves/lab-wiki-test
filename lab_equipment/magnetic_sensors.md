# Magnetic Sensors

Here is a list of magnetic sensors we currently have or have used in the lab.

---
## Handheld-Use Magnetometers

- [Metrolab THM1176-LF](Metrolab_THM1176-LF.md) three-axis Hall magnetometer
- [Teensy-based magnetometer][teensy-mag-repo] three-axis magnetometer
- [Honeywell HMR2300][docs] three-axis magnetometer **(Obsolete)**
- [Hirst GM08][docs] single-axis magnetometer **(Broken)**

---
## Discrete ICs

**Allegro Sensors**

- [A1301 and A1302][docs] ratiometric single-axis linear Hall-effect sensor (discontinued)
- [A139x][docs] micro power 3V single-axis linear Hall-effect sensor

**Melexis Sensors**

- [MLX90393][docs] three-axis Hall-effect sensor

**Infineon**

- [TLV493D-A1B6][docs] three-axis Hall-effect sensor (used on the Teensy-based Magnetometer)
